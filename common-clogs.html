Common clogs

Fast help for the drains people use every day

Choose the problem that looks familiar and book a visit before a slow drain becomes a full backup.

01

Clogged Bathtub Drains

Hair, soap scum, and old buildup can leave standing water after every shower or bath.

02

Clogged Dishwashers

Drain hose problems, disposal clogs, or kitchen line buildup can stop dishwasher drainage.

03

Clogged Shower Drains

Slow showers are often early warnings before water reaches the bathroom floor.

04

Clogged Sinks

Kitchen grease, food scraps, toothpaste, hair, and small objects are common causes.

05

Clogged Toilets

Repeated toilet clogs can point to a fixture issue, a branch line clog, or a main line problem.

06

Clogged Washing Machines

Lint, debris, and standpipe restrictions can create laundry-area overflows.

07

Clogged Floor Drains

Basements, garages, utility rooms, and commercial spaces need reliable floor drainage.

08

Clogged Exterior Drains

Leaves, mud, roots, and storm debris can overwhelm driveway and landscape drains.

Before you call

Helpful details to share with the dispatcher

Tell Gianni where the backup is happening, when it started, whether more than one fixture is affected, and whether water or sewage is surfacing anywhere outside.

One fixtureOften a local clog or fixture trap issue.
Several fixturesCould point to a branch or main drain problem.
Outdoor overflowMay need emergency sewer line attention.
